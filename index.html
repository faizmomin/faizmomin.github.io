Hey There

How much social media do you use? Have you thought about how much information you put on the internet about yourself? Do you know what cyber bullying is? If you don't, you are in the right place. Here you will find out what bullying is, what cyber bullying is, the different people involved in it, and tips on how you can prevent it.

Bullying

So what is bullying? In the oxford dictionary, it says "Use superior strength or influence to intimidate (someone), typically to force them to do something". This pretty much means that somebody thinks that they are better that someone else and that they can do what they want to the person. The bully uses their power (this can be strength, popularity, etc) to force another person to do something else. Depending on how serious the problem is, the consequences can be fatal for the victim.
