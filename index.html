How to Play

The goal of surviv.io is to be the last player standing. You only live once per game - there is no respawn!

2D PUBG

If you've played other battle royale games like PUBG, Fortnite or H1Z1, then you're already halfway there! Think of surviv.io as 2D PUBG (with slightly less desync and more chicken).

Loot and Kill

You'll begin the game with no items other than a simple backpack. Move around the map to find loot: weapons, ammo, scopes, and medical items. Eliminate other players and you can take their loot!

Red = Bad!

Players aren't the only thing that can hurt you. The deadly red zone will move in from the sides of the map and deal increasingly greater damage if you stand in it. Keep an eye on the map and stay safe.

Mar. 14, 2018

Survivrs assemble!

Squads have arrived.

Gather up your three best friends (or the three best players amongst your friends), and enter into the controlled chaos that is squad mode. You don't know true fear until you've been pursued and shot at by four angry yellow circles.

We've also spruced up the leaderboard to allow sorting by game mode. The "Most kills" category will display all the members of the team that contributed to the kill total for that game. All other categories will show individual prowess only.

Finally, we gave our client a much-needed performance boost. We hear you guys loud and clear, and will continue to work on upgrading our client to create the best experience possible.

We hope you all enjoy squad mode! If you're looking for teammates, look no further than our marvelous Discord server!

Mar. 2, 2018

Home invasion

Home is where the loot is with the arrival of two new buildings, two new building features and FIVE new obstacle types!

The barn and red house feature the latest in home construction technology: windows and doors. Windows can be broken and shot through, but cannot be moved through. Doors can be opened by pressing F, or by punching them.

These two buildings also contain several new furnishings. Drawers and cabinets can be destroyed for their precious loot. Tables can be hidden underneath for a sneaky home ambush. The refrigerator and oven reflect bullets, but beware the oven! Gas leaks are no joking matter.

We've also added emote customization. Click the surviv.io icon next to the name input field to access the emote loadout menu. Additional emotes can be unlocked by following us on Twitter, subscribing to our YouTube channel or liking us on Facebook. If you've already performed one of these actions, simply click the unlock button and the emotes will unlock.

In addition to the main emote wheel, players can customize two triggered emotes: on-win (chicken) and on-death (skull and crossbones).

Need a place to ask when squads are coming? Try our Discord server!

5.56x45mm ... x4

Move over 7.62, there's a new sheriff in town. The good folks at the surviv.io armory have distributed not one, not two, not three, but FOUR new guns chambered for the 5.56 NATO round.

First up is the HK416 assault rifle. If you like the MAC-10, well, meet his roided up bigger brother. The HK416 may not achieve the insane cyclic rates of the MAC-10, but it packs a harder punch.

Next we've got the FAMAS bullpup assault rifle. It's a burst-fire only weapon with a 25-round magazine. Accuracy will be at a premium, but if you can land your shots, this puppy will take a serious bite out of the competiton.
